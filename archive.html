GROUP CATNIPS

Things we love:

  • Older narrator reflecting on childhood
  • Ambiguous/open endings
  • Devastating final paragraphs
  • Stories that reward re-reading
  • Short enough to read twice (under 20 pages)
  • "Increasing bewilderment" (Fanny Howe via Hannah)

Things to avoid: Skinning of human beings (<1% target), stories too long to read twice, "casually cruel" writing with no payoff, French references as "award ploy" (John)
